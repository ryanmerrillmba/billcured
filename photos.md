# BillCured.com - Complete Photo & Asset Guide

This document contains all visual assets needed for the BillCured website. Use this guide with Gemini to find stock photos, or with Nano Banana to generate images.

**Visual Style:** Warm & Authentic - Real-looking people, natural lighting, candid moments that build trust. The target audience is stressed about medical bills, so imagery should be empathetic but empowering.

**Brand Colors for Reference:**
- Primary Teal: #2B6B7C
- CTA Salmon: #FA8072
- Background: #FAFBFC

## NOTES FOR AI IMAGE FINDING/CREATION

1. **Consistency is key** - All images should feel like they belong to the same website
2. **Diverse representation** - Include people of different ages, ethnicities, and backgrounds
3. **Avoid clich√©s** - No images of people literally crying over bills or looking devastated
4. **Empowering tone** - People should look determined, hopeful, or relieved - not defeated
5. **Natural lighting** - Warm, natural light works best for the "authentic" feel
6. **High resolution** - All source images should be at least 2x the display size for retina screens
